The Craftsmanship

The Ethos Couture Asymmetric Wrap Gown is a testament to the Digital Atelier's commitment to structural elegance. Crafted from 40mm double-faced silk satin, each piece undergoes a 12-hour hand-draping process in our Milanese workshop.

The architecture of the neckline provides a sharp contrast to the fluid movement of the biased-cut skirt, creating a silhouette that is both grounded and ethereal.
